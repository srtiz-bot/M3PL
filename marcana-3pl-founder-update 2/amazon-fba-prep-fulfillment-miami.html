What changed

What Amazon changed on January 1, 2026

Amazon discontinued the prep services sellers relied on. The work didn't disappear — it moved to you.

FNSKU labeling — discontinued

Amazon no longer applies your FNSKU barcodes. Every unit must arrive already labeled, or it can't be received.

Poly-bagging — discontinued

Bags with the required suffocation warning are now your responsibility — done wrong, units get blocked.

Bubble-wrapping — discontinued

Fragile-item protection is no longer Amazon's job. Improper prep means damage with no reimbursement.

No prep = no entry.

Right now, units arriving unprepped are refused or returned at your expense — and Amazon won't reimburse the damage. A prep partner isn't optional anymore.
